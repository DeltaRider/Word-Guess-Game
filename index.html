Hood Rap Hangman!

The hip-hop/rap themed hangman game.

Here's how to play:

You will be given a hint about a hip-hop/rap song, artist, or fact. Keep in mind these ain't your momma's rap songs, artists, or facts. This game only includes the realest.

With the hint, you will try to guess the word or phrase, entering one letter at a time. The amount of letters in the word or phrase is represented by underscores _ _ _ _ _ _ _.

If you guess the letter correclty, the underscore will be filled in with that letter.

If you guess incorrectly, the man will begin the process of hanging. You have 9 tries before he is fully hung. A fully hung sucka means you lose, sucka.

If you guess every letter in the word before he is hung then I guess it's your lucky day...

Press the spacebar to begin!
